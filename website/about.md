# About Management & Marketing

## Journal Overview

*Management & Marketing: Challenges for Knowledge Society* is an international, peer-reviewed, open access journal published by the Faculty of Marketing at Bucharest University of Economic Studies, Romania. Established to advance scholarly discourse in business and management disciplines, the journal serves as a vital platform for innovative research addressing contemporary challenges in the global knowledge economy.

## History & Mission

Since its inception, our journal has been committed to:

- **Advancing Knowledge**: Publishing high-quality research that contributes to theoretical development and practical application
- **Global Reach**: Fostering international collaboration and diverse perspectives
- **Open Access**: Ensuring free and unrestricted access to scholarly knowledge
- **Interdisciplinary Dialogue**: Bridging management, marketing, economics, and related disciplines
- **Practical Impact**: Connecting academic research with business practice and policy-making

## Publishing Partnership

We are proud to partner with **Springer Nature**, one of the world's leading research publishers, ensuring:

- Professional publishing standards
- International visibility and discoverability
- Technical infrastructure and support
- Alignment with best practices in scholarly publishing

This partnership enhances our ability to serve the global research community while maintaining our commitment to open access principles.

## Open Access Model

All articles in *Management & Marketing* are published under the **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0)**. This means:

### Benefits for Readers
- Free, immediate, and permanent access to all published content
- No subscription barriers or paywalls
- Freedom to read, download, print, and share

### Benefits for Authors
- Maximum visibility and discoverability
- Higher citation rates and research impact
- Compliance with open access mandates
- Retention of copyright with liberal reuse rights

### Funding Model
Our open access publishing is supported by:
- Author publication charges (with waivers available)
- Institutional support from Bucharest University of Economic Studies
- Partnership arrangements with Springer Nature

## Quality Standards

### Peer Review Process
Every submission undergoes rigorous double-blind peer review by international experts in relevant fields. Our process ensures:

- Objective evaluation of scholarly merit
- Constructive feedback to authors
- Transparency and integrity in editorial decisions
- Timely review and publication

### Editorial Oversight
Our distinguished international editorial board provides guidance on:
- Journal scope and strategic direction
- Manuscript evaluation and selection
- Special issue development
- Maintaining publication ethics

### Publication Ethics
We adhere to internationally recognized standards including:
- COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics) guidelines
- Plagiarism detection for all submissions
- Conflict of interest disclosure requirements
- Research ethics and data sharing policies
- Correction and retraction procedures

## FAQ

**Q: Is there a submission fee?**
A: No, we do not charge for manuscript submission. Publication charges may apply for accepted articles, with waivers available.

**Q: How long is the review process?**
A: We aim for initial decisions within 8-12 weeks, though this varies by article complexity and reviewer availability.

**Q: Can I submit a manuscript in progress?**
A: We only accept completed manuscripts ready for peer review. Pre-submission inquiries are welcome.

**Q: Do you publish special issues?**
A: Yes, we welcome proposals for thematic special issues. Contact our editorial office for guidelines.

**Q: How do I access published articles?**
A: All articles are freely available on our website and through our publishing partners without registration or subscription.
