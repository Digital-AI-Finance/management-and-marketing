# Aims & Scope

## Mission Statement

*Management & Marketing: Challenges for Knowledge Society* is an international, peer-reviewed, open access journal dedicated to advancing theoretical and empirical knowledge in management, marketing, economics, and related business disciplines. We provide a platform for innovative research that addresses contemporary challenges in the knowledge economy and contributes to both academic discourse and practical application.

## Focus Areas

Our journal welcomes original research articles, review papers, and case studies in the following areas:

### Marketing

- **Consumer Behavior**: Decision-making processes, psychological aspects of consumption, digital consumer behavior
- **Digital Marketing**: Social media marketing, content marketing, influencer marketing, marketing analytics
- **Brand Management**: Brand equity, brand positioning, corporate branding, brand loyalty
- **Marketing Strategy**: Market segmentation, targeting, positioning, competitive strategy
- **International Marketing**: Cross-cultural marketing, global branding, emerging markets

### Management

- **Strategic Management**: Corporate strategy, competitive advantage, strategic decision-making
- **Organizational Behavior**: Leadership, organizational culture, employee motivation, team dynamics
- **Human Resource Management**: Talent management, performance management, organizational development
- **Innovation Management**: Innovation strategies, R&D management, technology adoption
- **Knowledge Management**: Organizational learning, intellectual capital, knowledge sharing

### Economics & Business

- **Business Economics**: Market structures, industrial organization, firm behavior
- **Entrepreneurship**: New venture creation, entrepreneurial ecosystems, startup management
- **Corporate Governance**: Board effectiveness, stakeholder management, business ethics
- **Supply Chain Management**: Logistics, operations management, value chain optimization
- **Sustainability**: Corporate social responsibility, sustainable business practices, circular economy

### Emerging Topics

- **Artificial Intelligence in Business**: AI applications, machine learning in marketing, algorithmic decision-making
- **Digital Transformation**: Business model innovation, platform economics, digital ecosystems
- **Social Media & Society**: Social commerce, online communities, digital word-of-mouth
- **Data Analytics**: Big data, business intelligence, predictive analytics
- **Post-Pandemic Business**: Remote work, resilience, crisis management

## Methodological Approaches

We welcome diverse research methodologies, including:

- Quantitative studies (surveys, experiments, econometric analyses)
- Qualitative research (case studies, ethnographies, interviews)
- Mixed methods approaches
- Systematic literature reviews and meta-analyses
- Conceptual and theoretical papers
- Computational and simulation studies

## Target Audience

Our journal serves:

- Academic researchers in business and management
- Graduate students and doctoral candidates
- Business practitioners and consultants
- Policy makers and regulatory bodies
- Anyone interested in contemporary business challenges

## Open Access Commitment

All articles are published under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0), ensuring:

- Free and immediate access to all content
- Freedom to read, download, copy, and distribute
- Proper attribution to authors
- Maximum visibility and impact for research

## Publication Ethics

We adhere to the highest standards of publication ethics following:

- COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics) guidelines
- Rigorous peer review process
- Plagiarism detection for all submissions
- Transparency in editorial decisions
- Conflict of interest disclosure requirements

## Why Publish With Us?

- **Global Reach**: Wide international readership and author base
- **Open Access**: Maximum visibility and citation impact
- **Fast Publication**: Efficient review and publication process
- **Quality Standards**: Rigorous peer review by field experts
- **Indexing**: Listed in major academic databases
- **Author Support**: Comprehensive guidance throughout the publication process
